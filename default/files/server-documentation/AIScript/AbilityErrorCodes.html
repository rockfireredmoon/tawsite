Reference: Ability Error Codes

Purpose

A listing of ability error codes. Consult this char if ServerConfig.txt DebugLogAIScriptUse is enabled, to determine why an ability may not be activating.

Codes 0 through 4 are used for internal states and core error handling (like if the ability doesn't exist).

Codes 5 and beyond are usually a failure to pass the required activation conditions (facing target, in range, enough might, etc).

Return Codes

ABILITY_SUCCESS 0 No error code.
ABILITY_NOT_FOUND 1 The ability was not found in the loaded ability table.
ABILITY_BAD_EVENT 2 The ability has an invalid event type.
ABILITY_NOT_SPECIAL 3 The ability does not have a hack for special internal processing.
ABILITY_SPECIAL 4 The ability does have a hack for special internal processing (special case just needs to have a different value than ABILITY_NOT_SPECIAL for comparison purposes).
ABILITY_GENERIC 5 Non-specific failure.
ABILITY_NO_TARGET 6 No targets found.
ABILITY_COOLDOWN 7 Ability is still on cooldown and can't be used yet.
ABILITY_PENDING 8 An ability is already being processed.
ABILITY_FACING 9 Not facing target.
ABILITY_INRANGE 10 Not within range of target.
ABILITY_NOTSILENCED 11 The caster is silenced.
ABILITY_MIGHT 12 Not enough might to cast.
ABILITY_WILL 13 Not enough will to cast.
ABILITY_MIGHT_CHARGE 14 Not enough might charges to cast.
ABILITY_WILL_CHARGE 15 Not enough will charges to cast.
ABILITY_HASMAINHAND 16 No mainhand weapon equipped.
ABILITY_HASOFFHAND 17 No offhand weapon equipped.
ABILITY_HASMELEE 18 No melee weapon equipped.
ABILITY_HASSHIELD 19 No shield equipped.
ABILITY_HASBOW 20 No bow equipped.
ABILITY_HAS2HORPOLE 21 No two-hand or pole weapon equipped.
ABILITY_HASWAND 22 No wand equipped.
ABILITY_STATUS 23 The status effect condition does not match the requirement (commonly HasStatus() or NotStatus() functions)
ABILITY_HEALTH_TOO_LOW 24 The caster does not have enough health to use the ability.
ABILITY_BEHIND 25 The caster is not behind its target.
ABILITY_NEARBY_SANCTUARY 26 The caster is not near a sanctuary.
ABILITY_DISARM 27 The caster is disarmed and cannot perform physical attacks.
ABILITY_STUN 28 The caster is stunned.
ABILITY_DAZE 29 The caster is dazed.
ABILITY_DEAD 30 The caster is dead.
